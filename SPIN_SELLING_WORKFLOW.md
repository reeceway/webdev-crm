## üìû Stage 2: Meeting (Audit Presentation)

### Auto-Created Tasks:

| Day | Task | What To Do |
|-----|------|------------|
| 0 | **Schedule audit presentation call** | "I'd love to show you the full audit results - it's eye-opening. 20 minutes?" |
| 1 | **Prepare SPIN questions** | Based on audit, create questions:<br>- Problem: "Your mobile score is 35/100 - are you seeing customers bounce?"<br>- Implication: "With 70% of searches on mobile, how many potential customers are you losing?"<br>- Need-Payoff: "If we could get that to 90+, how would that impact your bottom line?" |
| 1 | **Create audit presentation** | Build deck/PDF:<br>- Current state (screenshots, scores)<br>- Business impact (lost customers, lost revenue)<br>- Cost of inaction (competitors winning)<br>- Opportunity (what's possible) |
| 2 | **Send meeting invite** | Subject: "Free Website Audit Review - Discover Hidden Issues Costing You Customers"<br>Include agenda in description |

### The Audit Presentation Call:

**Structure (20-30 mins):**

1. **Intro (2 min)**
   - Thanks for time
   - "I ran an audit and found some concerning issues affecting your business"

2. **Current State - Situation Questions (5 min)**
   - Walk through audit results
   - "Your site scored 45/100. Were you aware of these issues?"
   - "How long have you had this website?"
   - "Who handles your online marketing?"

3. **Problems - Problem Questions (5 min)**
   - Highlight specific issues from audit
   - "Your site has no SSL - Google marks it 'Not Secure'"
   - "You're missing meta descriptions on all pages"
   - "Page speed is 4.2 seconds - industry standard is under 2"
   - **Ask:** "Are you experiencing low traffic? High bounce rates?"

4. **Business Impact - Implication Questions (8 min)**
   - **This is the key SPIN stage**
   - "Let me show you what this means for your business..."
   - "Google penalizes sites without HTTPS - you're likely not ranking for your main keywords. How does that affect your lead flow?"
   - "With a 4-second load time, you're losing 40-50% of visitors before they see your services. If you get 100 visitors/day, that's 40-50 potential customers gone. What's your average customer worth?"
   - "Your competitors with faster, mobile-friendly sites are capturing those customers. How does that make you feel?"
   - **Quantify the pain:** "At $2,000 average sale and 10% conversion, you're leaving $8,000-10,000/month on the table"

5. **Solution Opportunity - Need-Payoff Questions (10 min)**
   - "What if we could fix all these issues?"
   - "Imagine your site loading in under 2 seconds, ranking on page 1 for your top keywords, and converting 2x more visitors. What would that do for your revenue?"
   - "If we could get you 50% more qualified leads per month, would that be worth an investment?"
   - **Get them to state the value:** "So if we could generate even 10 extra customers/month, that's $20,000 in revenue. Correct?"

6. **Next Steps (2 min)**
   - "I can put together a custom proposal that addresses each of these issues"
   - "Would you like to see what a solution looks like?"
   - **Move to Proposal stage**

### Key Proposal Elements:

**1. Tie Everything to the Audit**
- Every section references specific audit findings
- Use exact data from audit report (scores, load times, etc.)

**2. Use Their Numbers**
- Use the numbers they gave you in SPIN questions
- "You mentioned 100 visitors/day at 10% conversion..."
- "Based on your $2,000 average sale..."

**3. Show ROI**
- "Investment: $8,000"
- "Expected return (conservative): 10 extra customers/month = $20,000/month = $240,000/year"
- "ROI: 3,000% first year"

**4. Address All Audit Issues**
- Don't sell a "website" - sell "solutions to the problems we found"
- Each deliverable = a fix for an audit issue

## üí° Pro Tips for SPIN Selling with Audits

### 1. **The Audit is Your Ammunition**
- Every SPIN question references audit data
- "The audit shows X..." ‚Üí Makes it objective, not your opinion

### 2. **Quantify Everything**
- Slow speed = X% visitor loss
- Visitor loss √ó traffic √ó conversion = $ lost
- Make the pain tangible and mathematical

### 3. **Use Implication Questions Heavily**
- This is where SPIN selling shines
- "What happens if your competitors fix these issues and you don't?"
- "How much revenue could you lose over the next year if nothing changes?"

### 4. **Let Them Tell You the Value**
- Don't tell them what it's worth - ask
- "If we could generate 10 more leads per month, what would that be worth to you?"
- They convince themselves

### 5. **The Gift Creates Reciprocity**
- People feel obligated to hear you out after receiving a gift
- Makes the follow-up call much warmer

### 6. **Save Every Audit to Notes**
- Creates a paper trail
- Easy to reference during calls
- Shows professionalism
